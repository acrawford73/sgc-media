Playlists

A Playlist is an ordered list of videos that may contain a mix of videos for Movies, Series, Short-Form Videos, or TV Shows. It is a list of Video IDs that are defined elsewhere in the content feed. The same video can be included in multiple playlists. Playlists are similar to Tags: they help define the content that a channel's categories will display. The main difference is that playlists enable the order of the content to be manually specified; therefore, playlists are ideal for creating a "Featured" Category in a channel.
